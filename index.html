About Us

Welcome to Anderson's Tech Solutions, your trusted local electronics repair service located in Oak Grove, Missouri! As a proudly independent business, we specialize in repairing PCs, laptops, gaming consoles, and mobile devices for all our neighbors—young and old alike. We understand that technology plays a vital role in your life, whether you're gaming on your custom-built PC or staying connected with loved ones on your phone. That's why we're committed to providing fast, reliable, and personalized repair solutions right at your doorstep. With no storefront to limit us, we offer the convenience of coming to you for on-site repairs or picking up your device for service at our dedicated workspace. From minor fixes to complex custom computer builds, we handle every job with expertise and care. At Anderson's Tech Solutions, we’re not just repairing devices—we’re helping our community stay connected. Let us take the stress out of tech troubles and keep your devices running like new!
